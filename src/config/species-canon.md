# SPECIES Canon
## The Simple Guide for Understanding SPECIES

**Website:** https://species.market

---

## What Is SPECIES?

SPECIES is a demonstration marketplace that lives at species.market. It shows how a digital asset marketplace can work on the Onli platform. Think of it like a flight simulator — it looks and feels like the real thing, but you are not actually flying a plane. SPECIES looks and feels like a real marketplace, but it is a teaching tool.

The marketplace deals in a digital asset called **Specie**. Specie is a micro-commodity — a small digital asset that you own by holding it, the same way you own a dollar bill by having it in your hand. No account balances, no ledger entries, no middlemen. You hold it, you own it.

---

## What Is SPECIES For?

SPECIES exists for one reason: to teach. It is the flagship example of what an **Onli Appliance** can be. An Appliance is simply a marketplace that a developer builds and runs on the Onli platform.

SPECIES shows people — developers, partners, curious learners — exactly how such a marketplace works from end to end. How assets get created, how they move between people, how payments get verified, how AI assistants can help users manage their assets through conversation. It is the working model that makes all of those ideas concrete and understandable.

---

## Does SPECIES Have Real-World Value?

**No.** Specie has no real-world monetary value. It is not an investment. It is not a cryptocurrency. It is not something you buy to make money. It is a simulation asset that exists purely for educational and demonstration purposes.

The $1 price point is intentional — it signals that this is about learning, not speculation. You are not buying value; you are experiencing how a possession-based digital economy works.

---

## Why Use SPECIES to Learn?

Because it is the simplest way to understand a big idea.

The big idea behind Onli is that digital things can be owned the same way physical things are — by holding them. No blockchain, no public record of every transaction, no company sitting between you and your stuff. SPECIES takes that idea and turns it into something you can actually touch and use.

By interacting with SPECIES, you learn how digital assets can be issued, transferred, and redeemed without any of the complexity that blockchain systems require. You learn what "possession-based ownership" actually feels like. And if you are a developer, you get a complete blueprint for building your own marketplace on Onli.

---

## How Is SPECIES Different from Crypto?

SPECIES is not crypto. Here are the key differences in plain language:

- **No blockchain.** There is no public ledger recording every transaction. Your activity is private.
- **No mining or gas fees.** Transactions do not cost anything to process on a network.
- **No volatility.** Specie is fixed at $1. It does not go up or down.
- **No custodians.** Nobody else holds your assets for you. You hold them directly.
- **No complexity.** You do not need to understand wallets, keys, or seed phrases.

## Where Does SPECIES Fit in the Onli World?

**Onli** is the underlying technology — the protocol that makes possession-based digital assets possible.

**Onli One** (onli.one) is the network that connects everyone.

**Onli.You** is the free app where everyday users hold and manage their assets.

**Onli Cloud** is where developers go to build their own marketplaces (Appliances).

**SPECIES** (species.market) is the first Appliance — the example marketplace that shows how it all comes together.

**Specie** is the demonstration asset traded within SPECIES.

---

## Key Terms to Know

- **Specie** — The digital asset in the SPECIES marketplace. A micro-commodity owned through possession.
- **Appliance** — A marketplace built on the Onli platform. SPECIES is an example of one.
- **Micro-commodity** — A small digital asset that works like physical property. Not a token, not a coin.
- **Possession** — You own it because you hold it. No intermediary manages it for you.
- **Trust Without Chains** — The core idea: digital value can be verified and transferred without blockchains or middlemen.
